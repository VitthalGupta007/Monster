Privacy

Delete your data

VX Monster by VitthalX Studio — package com.vitthalxstudios.monster. See also our Privacy Policy.

Last updated: 11 July 2026

Important

VX Monster does not create a VitthalX Studio account. When you sign in with Google Play Games, progress can sync to your Google Play account via Google’s Saved Games service. Purchases and ads are handled by Google on your device’s Google account.

Delete local data on your device

  1. On your Android device: Settings → Apps → VX Monster → Storage → Clear data, or uninstall the app.
  2. That removes local progress, settings, mid-run saves, and cached data on that device.

Delete Google Play Games cloud save

Cloud progress is stored in Google Play Games Saved Games, not on VitthalX servers.

  1. Open the Google Play Games app → your profile → Your data in Play Games (or Google account Play Games data settings).
  2. Remove saved data for VX Monster, or delete Play Games activity for this game as offered by Google.
  3. Clearing app data on one device does not automatically delete cloud saves on other devices until you remove Play Games data or overwrite with a new cloud save after sign-in.

Request help by email

  1. Email vitthalgupta007@gmail.com with subject VX Monster data deletion request.
  2. Include your Google Play account email and whether you want help with local data, Play Games cloud save, or both.
  3. We aim to respond within 30 days.

What is deleted vs kept

  • Deleted by Clear data / uninstall: local game saves and settings on that device.
  • Managed by Google (not stored on VitthalX servers): Play Games sign-in, cloud saves, leaderboards, achievements, Play purchase history, Advertising ID / ad activity (AdMob). Use Google account / Play Games / Play / Ads settings to manage those.
  • May be retained by Google when required: purchase records for legal / accounting purposes under Google Play’s policies.
