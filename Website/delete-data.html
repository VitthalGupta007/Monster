Delete your data

VX Monster by VitthalX Studio — package com.vitthalxstudios.monster

Last updated: 11 July 2026

Important

VX Monster does not create a VitthalX Studio account. Game progress is stored on your device (local save file). Purchases and ads are handled by Google on your device’s Google account.

How to delete your game data (local)

  1. On your Android device: Settings → Apps → VX Monster → Storage → Clear data, or uninstall the app.
  2. That removes local progress, settings, mid-run saves, and local personal bests.

Request help by email

  1. Email vitthalgupta007@gmail.com with subject VX Monster data deletion request.
  2. Include your Google Play account email and that you want help deleting VX Monster data.
  3. We aim to respond within 30 days.

What is deleted vs kept

  • Deleted by Clear data / uninstall: local game saves and settings on that device.
  • Managed by Google (not stored on VitthalX servers): Play purchase history, Advertising ID / ad activity (AdMob), and any Google account data. Use Google account / Play / Ads settings to manage those.
  • May be retained by Google when required: purchase records for legal / accounting purposes under Google Play’s policies.
